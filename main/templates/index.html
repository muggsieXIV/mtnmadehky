About Mtn Made Hky

Mountain Made Hky provides young athletes the opportunity to greatly improve their skills through our on and off ice programs.

Using our professional experience and extensive knowledge of the game, we teach young athletes what is necessary for success at all levels of hockey.

Mtn Made Hky Staff

Elite coaches, from elite backgrounds.

Contact Coach Knox
Tim Knox

Co-Founder


Coach Knox grew up playing hockey and soccer in Fargo, ND. After graduating highschool he enlisted into the Marine Corps. His time serving our nation taught Coach Knox a complete understanding in not only the value, but the wisdom to teach discipline, teamwork, workethic, attention to detail, and the need for continual improvement.


After retiring from the service, Coach Knox began his career as a hockey coach. His hockey knowledge, military values, and soccer influenced off ice training, quickly proved to be successful for developing elite level athletes.


After many seasons in North Dakota, Coach Knox relocated to Colorado were he developed the Renegades out of Fort Collins, CO. During the off seasons, Coach Knox ran summer camps in Omaha and Des Moines. After many successful seasons, Coach Knox merged the Renagades with NCYH, where he'd coach before making a move down to Denver with Evolution AAA. Coach Knox has also coached the past two seasons at the U12 and U13 levels with the Roughriders AAA in Superior, CO.


Some of the elite players Coach Knox trained through the years are Tyler Pham, Jackson Barlent, CJ Smith, Patrick Grasso, Levi Webber, Lance Pennington, Aiden Thompson, Eric Brink, Lowden Houg, Collin Munson, Ben Walker, Jack Walker, and Sammy Walker.

Contact Coach Walker
Ben Walker

Co-Founder


Ben is a retired Professional Hockey Player from Fargo, ND. He moved to Edina, MN at the age of seven where he grew up skating under the Minnesota Made Hockey program run by Bernie McBain. While playing for the Edina Hornets High School team his team won the state tournament in 2010. As a Senior, Ben moved to Victoria as a high school senior to join the Victoria Royals of the Western Hockey League. After three seasons in the (WHL) Ben was named Fan Favorite and received the Unsung Hero Award. Ben then signed a contract with the Winnipeg Jet's (AHL) affiliate the St. John's IceCaps. He re-located with the team to Winnipeg which became the Manitoba Moose, while also spending time with the Tulsa Oilers of the (ECHL). He then made a move to the Czech Republic where he signed with CEZ Motor Ceske Budejovice, before completing his career in Germany with EHC Freiburg.


In the off-seasons, Ben worked as an on ice coach with Minnesota Made Hockey in Edina, MN and a skills and fitness trainer at First Athlete in Edina, MN. Ben moved to CO in 2018, determined to teach young athletes his gained knowledge of the game. His goal is to provide young athletes on-ice training in skating, shooting, puck handling, and game awareness. He wants to use his experience to also provide athletes the crucial off-ice training needed to reach elite levels and play in junior, NCAA, and professional leagues. Ben has also assisted with the Rocky Mountain Roughriders AAA at the U12 and U16 (Colorado State Champions 2020) levels.
