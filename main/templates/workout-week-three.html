Week Three

Day One

Warm-Up


You will need a 20 yard space for this warm-up. Place two markers down. You will be using these markers to complete the exersizes.


If you do not have a short breath and sweat at the end of the warm up, do it again because you didn't do it right.


  1. Run to the marker and back 4 times no breaks. Effort per down and back 50%, 65%, 75%, 100%
  2. Toe Walks - Walk on your toes to the marker and back
  3. Heel Walks - Walk on your heels there and back
  4. Knee Hugs or Knee to Chest Walks, keep your shoulder blades back and traps down.
  5. Walking Quad Stretch, press through your toes, and reach to the sky with your opposite arm, really stretch your quads.
  6. Inverted Hamstring Stretch - Start off standing upright with shoulders back and chest up & out. Lift your right foot slightly off the floor. Without changing the angle of your left knee, bend your hips and lower your torso as far as you can. As you bend over, lift up your arms straight out to your sides until they are in line with your torso. Turn your palms to face up. Return back to the starting position.
  7. Rotational Lunge - Lunge forward, keep your front knee above your heel, and twist towards your up leg. Really get a hip flexor, and back stretch.
  8. Lateral Lunge - Take a medium size step with your right leg and keep your right foot facing forward. Slowly lower your hips in line with the right knee. Getting low, keep your hips back and holding this position for one to two seconds
  9. Inchworm - Keeping your legs locked, and torso flat, plant your feet and walk out to the push up position with your hands, do two pushups then walk forward with your feet, keeping your knees straight and back flat
  10. High Knees to the marker and back
  11. 5 squat hops in place, sprint through the marker.

Workout


Do this in a driveway, backyard, park. Somewhere with 20 yards of space. You will want to make four markers. First marker at the starting line, second marker at the 5 yard line, third at the 15 yard line, fourth marker at the 20 yard line. Complete each exercise at absolute top speed, and run through the finish line (DON’T QUIT EARLY).


  1. 5yard, walk back. 3x
  2. 15yard, walk back. 3x
  3. 20yard, walk back. 3x
  4. 15yard to 5yard to 20yard, walk back. 3x
  5. 20yard to 5yard to 15yard and back. 3x
  6. 20yard and back two times. 3x
  7. 5 yard and back three times. 3x
  8. 15 yard and back three times. 3x
  9. Forwards to 15, backwards to 5, sprint through 20, walk back. 3x
  10. Forwards to 5, backwards to 15, forwards to 20, backwards to 15, forwards to 5, backwards to start line. 3x (face the same direction every time you transition.)

Cool Down


Cool Downs prevent injury, and allow your body to recover faster. It also is a great opportunity to really work on your flexibility.


Each stretch you should hold for no less than 30 seconds.


Make sure you stretch out your Calves, Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings, Groin, Hip-Flexiors, Back, Shoulders, and Neck. Really work of your flexibility, and take long slow breathes.


It's a really good idea to drink 2 bottles of water, a gatorade, and a protein shake after any workout. Your body needs hydration, electrolytes, protein, sugars, vitamins, and carbohydrates to properly recover post workout. Nutritional timing is 30-60 minutes after any workout. Make sure you're taking care of your diet, and putting in what it needs to increase your optimum fitness.

Week Three

Day Two - Explode


Complete the warmup from Day One before you begin. Really push yourself


  1. Squat Hops - 25x. Keep your chest up, knees bent to 90, bring your knees to chest when you jump but keep your chest up the whole time
  2. Lunges - 25x each leg. Keep your chest up, your knee should be directly below your body and barely touching the ground when you lunge
  3. Bicycle Jumps - 10 each leg. Keep your chest up. Lunge, jump, and kick your legs in a full bicycle motion in the air, land on the same legs and finish with a lunge
  4. Pushup Walks. Pushup, then bear crawl one body length, turn 180, push up, then back. 25x
  5. Skaters. From your skating stance (90* knee bend chest up). Push out with one leg in your stride position. Bring it to full extension and back as fast as you can. 25x each leg.
  6. Speed Box. Up and down as fast as you can 15x. Use a curve, step, or box about 6-12 inches high.
  7. Lunges with a hop. Alternate legs in the air. 20x.
  8. Reverse Stands. 10x. Lay on your back. Keep your arms at your side and butt off the ground. With your arms and feet walk yourself to a seated position then sit down. Without your arms now stand straight up by pressing your feet into the floor. You’ll want to really engage your core so you can build momentum when pressing off the floor.
  9. Clappers. 20x. Pushup, clap your hands in the air.
  10. Step Up Jumps. Step on a box (6-12in) and press/drive through into a large jump straight in the air. Land on the ground and switch legs. 10 each leg.

Week Three

Day Three - Muscle Man


Complete the warmup from Day One before you begin. Ladder Drills. Each exercise should be executed as fast as you possibly can, but without losing control. The only way to get better is to be out of your comfort zone. 30 seconds on, 15 seconds off. Repeat circuit 3x. 120 seconds between sets.


  1. Push ups - 25
  2. Dips (do from a firm platform thats at least 2 ft off the ground, dont let your butt touch) - 25
  3. Knee Slaps (pushups, when you push up jump, and slap your knees with your hands. - 10
  4. Reverse V Up. From standing, keep your feet still, walk out with your hands into a push up, then walk back. 20
  5. Left Side V Up.
  6. Right Side V Up./li>
  7. 30 seconds, Swimmers (lay on your stomach, legs and shoulders off the ground. Arms straight out, Left arm up Right leg up, Right arm down Left leg down. Then alternate as fast as you can.
  8. Shuttle Run. place a cone or shoe at the starting line, 10 yard mark, and 5 yard mark. Full sprint the near, far back to near, back to far, finish at starting line.
  9. 20 yard sprint x 5
  10. Facing Left, two up, one back
  11. Facing Right, two up, one back

End circuit, repeat 3x. 120 seconds between sets.

Week Three

Day Four - Slow People Suck


Use tape, laces, a stick, or anything straight and flat. 30 seconds on, 30 seconds rest. Repeat circuit 3 times, 120 seconds rest in-between sets. Push yourself out of the comfort zone, but stay in control.


  1. Both feet over and back
  2. Both feet side to side
  3. Both feet, over 180 degrees back 180 degrees
  4. Two over two back vertically (feet move separately)
  5. Two over two back horizontally
  6. Single leg over and back vertically
  7. Single leg over and back vertically (other leg)
  8. Single leg over and back horizontally
  9. Single leg over and back horizontally (other leg)
  10. Right leg only: Up, Left, Back, Right. Move in a square.
  11. Left leg only: Up, Left, Back, Right. Move in a square.
  12. Right leg only: Face Left (length of line), Jump over right, Up, Left over line, Back.
  13. Left leg only: Face Left (length of line), Jump over right, Up, Left over line, Back.

( Repeat circuit 3x )


End circuit, repeat 3x. 120 seconds between sets.

Day Five - Finish Strong


Complete the warmup from Day One before you begin. Place markers in a diamond 10 yards apart from one another. Complete each exercise 5 times each, the 5 times the other direction. On #6, shuffle through the middle as drawn. Take 15-20 seconds between runs.



Post Circuit Wall sit challenge. Try to hit 10 minutes. Keep your back flat, knees bend to 90. Hands off your legs.
