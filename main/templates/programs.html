June 1-20 Summer Training Program

The off season is when champions are Made. Improve your hands, skating, shooting, passing, strength, and conditioning during our Monday - Friday all day Summer Training Program. Athletes will have the opportunity to skate during a 9am Ice Session, working on Puck Handling and Shooting. Followed by a dryland session that focuses on Agility, Speed, and Quickness. Athletes then break for lunch before a second dryland session working on Strength and Conditioning. Athletes finish their day with a 3pm on ice session working on Over Speed, Passing, and Competing Drills/Games.


Age Levels: U10 | U12 | U14
